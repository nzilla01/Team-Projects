# FinTrack — User Guide

## Getting Started

**Register** — click "Register" on the login page, fill in your name, email, and a password (6+ characters).

**Log In** — enter your email and password on the login page.

**Log Out** — click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the sidebar.

## Dashboard

Shows your total balance, total income, total expenses, your 5 most recent transactions, and a spending breakdown by category.

## Transactions

**Add** — click "+ Add Transaction", choose Income or Expense, enter amount, date, category, and description.

**Edit** — hover over a transaction and click the pencil icon.

**Delete** — hover over a transaction, click the trash icon, and confirm.

**Filter** — use the dropdown at the top to show only Income or only Expenses.

## Savings Goals

**Create a goal** — click "+ New Goal", enter a title, optional description, target amount, and target date.

**Add money** — type an amount in the deposit field on any goal card and click "Add".

**Completion** — once the saved amount reaches the target, the goal is automatically marked Completed.

**Delete** — click the trash icon on any active goal.

## Categories

10 categories are pre-loaded: Food & Dining, Rent & Housing, Transport, Entertainment, Healthcare, Education, Shopping (expense), and Salary, Freelance, Other Income (income).
